Privacy Policy for Megit

Last updated: June 15, 2024

1. Information We Collect

Megit collects your Google email and profile information via Google Sign-In to provide a personalized experience. We also request access to your YouTube Music data (read-only) to allow you to view your library and playlists within the app.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Authenticate your account.
  • Display your YouTube Music playlists and library.
  • Sync your settings across devices via Firebase.

3. Data Sharing

We do NOT share your personal data or YouTube information with any third-party companies. Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase.

4. YouTube Data Usage

Megit's use of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Service User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions, contact us via our GitHub repository.
